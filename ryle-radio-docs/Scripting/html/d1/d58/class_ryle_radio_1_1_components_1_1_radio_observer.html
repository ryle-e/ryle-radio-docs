enum RyleRadio.Components.RadioObserver.EventType

The event an observer is looking for.

Enumerator
Gain 

the volume of the track: gain * broadcast power * insulation

TunePower 

the gain of the track: this is combined from the gain variable on the track and the tuning power on the output

BroadcastPower 

the gain of the track: this is combined from the gain variable on the track and the tuning power on the output

Insulation 

the broadcast power of the track: how close to any active RadioBroadcasters the output is

TrackEnds 

the insulation of the track: the higher the value the less insulation- the power of any RadioInsulator the output is in

TrackStarts 

the track ends, or loops

OutputTune 

the track starts, or loops (happens after TrackEnds)

None 

the tune on the output is changed
